About Me

I'm a 21-year-old graphic designer with two years of professional experience. I have worked on social media designs for high-school teachers, worked in the sports field (football), and created YouTube thumbnails. My work focuses on clear messaging, strong typographic choices, and practical visual systems that perform across digital platforms.

I enjoy collaborating with clients to turn ideas into memorable visuals and I keep learning new techniques to grow my craft.
